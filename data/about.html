About Turtl

Turtl is an open-source note and bookmark (and soon file) storage app. Not only does it aim to be fast and useful, it encrypts all your data using your login/password before storing anything on the Turtl servers.

This means that nobody in the world, except you and those who you specifically share with, is able to see the contents of your data (not even the NSA, or any other government agency).

For more information, visit the Turtl website or browse the source.
